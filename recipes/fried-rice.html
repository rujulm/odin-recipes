Fried Rice

Description

Fried Rice is a popular dish that turns leftover rice into a flavorful and satisfying meal. Stir-fried with vegetables, eggs, and soy sauce, fried rice is a great way to create something delicious from basic ingredients. It’s a favorite across many cultures and kitchens.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Green onions
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  1. Scramble eggs in a pan with a little oil, then set aside.
  2. In the same pan, add more oil and sauté garlic.
  3. Add vegetables and cook until tender.
  4. Add rice, stir-fry everything together.
  5. Add soy sauce, stir well. Add scrambled eggs back in.
  6. Season with salt and pepper, garnish with green onions.
