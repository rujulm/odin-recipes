Shawarma

Description

Chicken Shawarma is a Middle Eastern street food classic known for its bold spices and tender, juicy meat. Traditionally cooked on a vertical rotisserie, this dish features thin slices of marinated chicken, typically wrapped in warm pita bread with fresh vegetables and sauces like garlic toum or tahini. It's beloved for its deep flavor and satisfying, portable format.

Home-style versions are easy to make using a hot skillet or oven. You still get that rich, spiced flavor with tools you already have. Chicken Shawarma can be served in wraps, bowls, or over rice. It's a flexible and flavorful choice for quick lunches or weeknight dinners.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast or thighs
  • 3 tbsp plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Pita bread
  • Optional toppings: lettuce, tomato, onion, garlic sauce or tahini

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, oil, and all the spices.
  2. Add the chicken, coat well, and marinate for at least 1 hour (overnight for best flavor).
  3. Cook the chicken in a hot pan or oven until fully cooked and slightly crispy.
  4. Warm pita bread and fill with chicken and your choice of toppings.
  5. Drizzle with garlic sauce or tahini. Roll and enjoy!
